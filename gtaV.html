10. State of Decay: Year One Survival Edition Apr 28

One of the hottest games of 2015! This game takes place in a virtual world where crimes are commonplace everyday. The player gets to do whatever he wants to his own character and create mayhem throughout the virtual world. The player has many quests to complete throughout his journey. This virtual world is pretty much a sandbox where the character can do whatever he wants.
Graphics
First off, the game's graphics are superb. The graphics are well established and they are surreal. You can adjust view-distances and field of view. Controls are customizable and you can play your own music library on the in game radio. There are 4k/UHD resolutions and multi display set-up options.
Controls and Game play
The controls are pretty self explanatory and easy to get use to. Like any other shooting games, you use the mouse to shoot and you move the mouse around to aim. The WASD keys are the typical keys used to move the character around. The game play is ridiculously fun if you like doing whatever you want in a virtual reality world. You can do pretty much anything in this game. This sandbox game is almost an escapade similar to the real world. Whether you want to oppose the authorities and become the king of criminal or you want to uphold your citizen duties and fight against gangs is all up to you. Since you are the player, you get to choose the actions outlining the future of your character and your game play style.
