One of the best games exclusively to the PS4! This game is probably the epitome of one of the best RPG games this year. This game takes place in the ancient city of Yharnam, now cursed with a strange endemic illness spreading through the streets like a disease. Dangers and death manifest this dark world. Your task is to uncover the dark secrets underlying the problem while surviving. You must utilize all your wits, strategies, skills, weapons, and reflexes to adventure through a tough yet fun time.
Graphics
Bloodborne's graphics are surreal and brilliant. This is amazing especially since the game is on a platform device, not a computer. Once the player enters the world of Bloodborne, the graphics are too intense and real. The textures and particles are all defined uniquely and incredibly. If anything, this game's graphics could be a model for future PS4 games incoming.
Controls and Game play
The controls are the PS4 controller. It is quite self explanatory as to how to play around with this game. There are not too many complex controls that limits the player from accessing anything. The gameplay is excellent as of so far. According to many other reviews, one will spend a long time playing through this game. There is almost 100+ hours of gameplay ready in Bloodborne. The player gets to adventure through difficulty dungeons and fight bosses at the end of them. On top of quests, they can upgrade perks and interact with NPCs and perform almost anything RPG games can do.
